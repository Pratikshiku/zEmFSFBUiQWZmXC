## Agricultural Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/71f928aa3f994f408f7ae32bd661df9a/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular Users, Employees, Administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Announcement Information
5. Crops
6. Weather Data
7. Products
8. Land Planning
9. Personal Center
10. Shopping Cart

**Employee Functions:**
1. Login
2. Change Password
3. Personal Information
4. Announcement Information Management
5. Product Management
6. Product Review Management
7. Product Order Management
8. Land Planning Management
9. Crop Management
10. Farm Work Schedule Management
11. Seedling & Breeding Management
12. Weather Data Management
13. Monitoring Node Management
14. Monitoring Record Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Basic Data Management (Announcement Type Management, Monitoring Node Types, Farm Work Schedule Types, Crop Types, Weather Data Types, Product Type Management, Land Type Management, Seedling & Breeding Types)
5. Announcement Information Management
6. Product Management
7. Product Review Management
8. Product Favorites Management
9. Product Order Management
10. Land Planning Management
11. Crop Management
12. Crop Message Management
13. Crop Favorites
14. Farm Work Schedule Management
15. Seedling & Breeding Management
16. Weather Data Management
17. Weather Data Messages
18. Weather Data Favorites
19. Monitoring Node Management
20. Monitoring Record Management
21. User Management
22. Employee Management
23. Banner Management
